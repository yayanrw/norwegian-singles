Long Run Field Card

Pre · During · Post — Norwegian Singles

☀️ Pre-Run 2–3 h before
Eat a proper carb meal Fuel
Rice + protein, bread, banana — avoid high fat/fibre
500 ml water + electrolytes Hydrate
Don't just drink plain water — include sodium
Pack fuel & salt for the run Sodium
Salt tabs / electrolyte sachets + gel or dates
Run early morning — before 7 am
Avoid afternoon heat in tropical climate (30°C+)
Start pace conservatively
Target 8:30–9:00 /km on hilly course — not 6:30
🏃 During Run Every 30–40 min
Take electrolytes every 30–40 min Sodium
Salt tab or electrolyte drink — especially on hot days
30–40 g carbs per hour Fuel
Gel, banana, dates — start at 30 min, don't wait
Drink before thirsty Hydrate
150–200 ml every 20 min in heat
Keep HR below 70% max
Walk hills if needed — easy pace is the goal
Check cadence drops
Below 160 spm = fatigue signal, ease off pace
✅ Post-Run Within 30–60 min
Rehydrate with electrolytes first Sodium
500–750 ml electrolyte drink — not plain water alone
Eat within 30 min Fuel
Carbs + protein — rice & egg, banana & milk
Continue drinking through the day Hydrate
Urine should be pale yellow — dark = still dehydrated
No quality session next 24–48 h
Long run is your stress — full recovery before next Q
Log HR, pace & how you felt
Flag any tingling, dizziness or nausea for next plan
🚨 Stop Immediately If...
Tingling spreads to face or feet Stop
Sit down · take salt + sugar + water now
Vision darkens or tunnels Stop
Lie down, legs elevated · call someone if alone
Nausea + dizziness together Stop
Classic heat exhaustion — shade, fluids, rest
Confusion or can't think clearly Stop
Heat stroke risk — seek help immediately
